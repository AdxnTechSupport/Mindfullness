Basic Needs

Eating Well: Eating healthy and eating when hungry is a essential to feeling good.

Staying Active: Physical activity allows the brain to cause chemical changes that can improve mood. It can also raise self esteem which in turn makes you feel better.

Being Social: Socailising is incredibly important to maintaining good mental wellbeing, especially in a post-Covid era. Being social allows you to make connections which in turn makgives you a sense of belonging. Being around others gives you a chance to recieve emotional support and it also allows you to help others who need it.

Academic Stress:

Academic stress is something that you are going to deal with alot throughout your time at post-secondary. So it's best that you know how to effectively manage it.

Plan Ahead: Knowing when a test is or when an assignment is due can save you from a lot of trouble! Put the dates of test and dues dates onto to the same calender so you can get an overview for all your classes.

Don't fall Behind: Falling behind on a class is a mistake that you do

Speak to Someone: Some post-secondary institutions provide consuling for their students, this is a great place for you to talk about how you fell and to get help while on campus. If what you are dealing with is an academic matter, ask your instructors for help and clarification or go to peer tutoring sessions.
